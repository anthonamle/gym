Workout Plan Recommendations

Full Body Workout: Ideal for beginners and those with limited time. It allows for frequent training of each muscle group, promoting muscle synthesis and growth.

Push Pull Legs Workout: Great for intermediate lifters. It provides a balanced approach, allowing for adequate recovery and targeting different muscle groups on different days.

Upper Lower Workout: Suitable for those looking to train four times a week. It offers a good balance between training frequency and recovery, promoting muscle growth and strength.
